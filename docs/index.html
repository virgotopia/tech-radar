What is a Tech Radar?

The Tech Radar is a list of technologies, complemented by an assessment result, called ring assignment. I use four rings with the following semantics:

  • MASTER — Technologies I have high confidence in and I appreciate having a high level of expertise with low risk associated. I expect to continue working with these technologies the future.
  • ADOPT — Technologies that I have good understanding of and I'm in the proccess of increasing my proficiency with them; overall I'm enthusiastically looking forward to work with.
  • ASSESS — Technologies that are promising and have clear potential value-add; technologies worth to invest time, research and prototyping efforts in.
  • HOLD — Technologies that I think are not (yet) worth to (further) invest in. I do not expect to work with in future projects.

What is the purpose?

https://github.com/virgotopia/tech-radar The Tech Radar is a tool to reflect my knowledge and experience in IT technologies and it's continuously evolving landscape.
